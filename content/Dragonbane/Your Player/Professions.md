# Professions

You lived a life before adventuring. Your **profession** sets your starting **trained skills** (pick six from the list), your **starting gear**, and (usually) your **heroic ability**. Roll or choose.

> **D10 Professions**
> 1 Artisan • 2 Bard • 3 Fighter (choose a path) • 4 Hunter • 5 Mage • 6 Mariner • 7 Merchant • 8 Scholar • 9 Thief • 10 Free Choice

---

## Artisan
Builders, smiths, coopers, wainwrights—the folk who make civilization work in Kelvin, Threshold, and the Highforge markets.

- **Key Attribute:** STR  
- **Skills (choose six):** Axes, Brawling, **Crafting**, Hammers, Knives, Sleight of Hand, Spot Hidden, Swords  
- **Heroic Ability:** *Master Blacksmith* **or** *Master Carpenter* **or** *Master Tanner*  
- **Starting Gear (roll D6):**  
  1–2 Warhammer (small), leather armor, blacksmith’s tools, torch, flint & tinder, d8 rations, d8 silver  
  3–4 Handaxe, leather armor, carpentry tools, rope, torch, flint & tinder, d8 rations, d8 silver  
  5–6 Knife, leather armor, tanner’s tools, lantern + oil, flint & tinder, d8 rations, d8 silver  

---

## Bard
Road-worn storytellers, minstrels, and rumor-mongers; welcome in Traladaran inns and Thyatian courts alike.

- **Key Attribute:** CHA  
- **Skills:** Acrobatics, Bluffing, Evade, Knives, **Languages**, **Myths & Legends**, **Performance**, **Persuasion**  
- **Heroic Ability:** *Musician*  
- **Starting Gear (D6):**  
  1–2 Lyre, knife, oil lamp + oil, flint & tinder, d6 rations, d8 silver  
  3–4 Flute, dagger, rope, torch, flint & tinder, d6 rations, d8 silver  
  5–6 Horn, knife, torch, flint & tinder, d6 rations, d8 silver

## Fighter (choose a path)
Steel for hire in the Borderlands and the alleys of Specularum. **Pick one path** below; it nudges your skills, kit, and heroic knack. All fighters may pick from the broader list, but each path adds focus.

**Core Fighter List (any path):** Axes, Bows, Brawling, Crossbows, Evade, Hammers, Spears, Swords  
**Default Heroic Ability:** *Veteran* (or the path’s option)

### Path A — **Soldier (Levy/Guard)**
Karameikan levy, town watch, or duke’s foot—disciplined ranks and shield work.  
- **Add this option to your skills list:** Shields (treated as Swords for parry effects), **Tactics** (#HomebrewDetail, INT)  
- **Heroic Ability (choose one):** *Veteran* **or** *Shield Wall* (#HomebrewDetail: when adjacent to an ally with shield, gain a boon to Defend).  
- **Starting Gear (D6):**  
  1–2 Spear, small shield, chain shirt, torch, d6 rations, d6 silver  
  3–4 Shortsword, light crossbow + 10 bolts, leather, torch, d6 rations, d6 silver  
  5–6 Long spear, studded leather, open helm, torch, d6 rations, d6 silver

### Path B — **Skirmisher (Ranger-lite)**
Border scouts and caravan guards who fight on the move.  
- **Add to skills list:** **Bushcraft**, **Sneaking**  
- **Heroic Ability:** *Skirmisher* (#HomebrewDetail: after a successful Evade, step 2 squares; no free attacks).  
- **Starting Gear (D6):**  
  1–2 Handaxe, short bow + 20 arrows, leather, rope, d8 rations  
  3–4 Spear, sling + bullets, leather, bedroll, d8 rations  
  5–6 Sword, small shield, leather, snare, d8 rations

### Path C — **Duelist (Urban Blade)**
Fencers and bravados of Specularum, hired blades for the houses Vorloi and Radu alike.  
- **Add to skills list:** **Persuasion**, **Sleight of Hand**  
- **Heroic Ability:** *Riposte* (#HomebrewDetail: once/round after a successful parry, make a free Knives or Swords attack with bane).  
- **Starting Gear (D6):**  
  1–2 Rapier (counts as Sword), dagger, leather, cloak, d6 rations  
  3–4 Shortsword, buckler (small shield), leather, lamp + oil, d6 rations  
  5–6 Sabre (Sword), fine clothes, leather, d6 rations, d10 silver

### Path D — **Marksman (Archer/Crossbow)**
Hunts bandits on the Duke’s roads; feared in palisade fights at Sukiskyn.  
- **Add to skills list:** **Spot Hidden**, **Awareness**  
- **Heroic Ability:** *Marksman* (#HomebrewDetail: when you Aim, ignore one range band penalty **or** cover penalty).  
- **Starting Gear (D6):**  
  1–2 Longbow + 20 arrows, knife, leather, d6 rations  
  3–4 Light crossbow + 20 bolts, shortsword, leather, d6 rations  
  5–6 Short bow + 30 arrows, spear, leather, d6 rations

> **Nickname (D6):** 1 Gravemaker • 2 Windthaw • 3 Coldsteel • 4 Wolfbane • 5 Redeye • 6 The Fearless

## Sidebar: Earning **Knighthood** in Karameikos (In-Play)
There is **no Knight profession at start**. In Karameikos, knighthood is a **title and duty**, not a background. Characters can **petition or be sponsored** after notable service (e.g., holding the line at a homestead, unmasking treachery in Specularum, or safeguarding pilgrims).  

**Examples of Orders/Patrons:**  
- **Order of the Griffon** (Church of Karameikos): pious martial arm; expects valor and defense of the faithful.  
- **Ducal Household Knights** (Duke Stefan): oath to the Duchy; frontier defense and diplomacy.  
- **Callarii Silver Riders** (#HomebrewDetail): elven-allied light cavalry for those who’ve earned the forest’s trust.  

> On receiving a knighthood, the GM may grant: a **bonded mount**, the **Guardian** heroic ability, access to **Riding** as trained (if not already), and obligations (quests, ceremonies, lawfulness tests).
